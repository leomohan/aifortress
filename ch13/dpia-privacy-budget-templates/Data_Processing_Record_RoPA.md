# Record of Processing Activities (RoPA)
## AI Fortress — Chapter 13 Template 13.F-3

**Standard:** GDPR Article 30  
**Purpose:** Maintain a written record of all AI-related personal data processing activities.
Required for organisations with ≥ 250 employees OR processing that is likely to result in
a risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects.
